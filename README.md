# Optimal Treatment Methods for Breast Cancer Patients and Involvement of the ESR1 Gene

**University of St Andrews | MT4561 The History and Future of Data | Sep–Nov 2024**

Statistical analysis exploring the relationship between ESR1 gene expression levels and breast cancer treatment outcomes.

## Overview

Used the NKI Breast Cancer Dataset to investigate whether gene expression levels of the estrogen receptor gene (ESR1) influence treatment efficacy across different therapeutic approaches.

### Methods
- Exploratory data analysis across treatment and gene-expression subgroups
- Polynomial regression to model non-linear expression–outcome relationships
- Pearson correlation to quantify associations
- Hypothesis testing to assess statistical significance of observed trends

### Findings
- Identified measurable trends in survival outlooks across gene-expression/treatment subgroups
- Results supported the hypothesis that ESR1 expression levels are associated with differential treatment efficacy
